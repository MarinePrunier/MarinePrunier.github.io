Hi, I'm Marine, a first year as a doctoral student in astrophysics at the University of Montreal. My research is centered around understanding the feedback of active galactic nuclei (AGN) on galaxy clusters. Under the guidance of Prof. Hlavacek-Larrondo, a specialist in AGN feedback in galaxy clusters, and Dr. Annalisa Pillepich at the Max Planck Institute in Heidelberg (Co-PI of TNG-Cluster), I am conducting a comprehensive comparative analysis of the observational signature of AGN feedback on galaxy clusters using the TNG-Cluster cosmological simulation (Illustris TNG). My academic journey has taken me to various European universities in Switzerland, Belgium, France, and now Canada.

I'm interested in very different reasearch topics in astrophysics but also in aerospace engineering and planetary science. For more information on my present and past research projects, please check out the RESEARCH tab and MARS MISSION.
